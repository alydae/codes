template for a new box:
<div class="box">
    <h1>subtitle</h1>
    <div class="t">
    </div>
</div>

the boxes are identical in size to each other. you can move them around, delete them all or you can change the number. earlier in the code, under .box, there's instructions on changing the number of boxes. 2-4 is recommended, and only 4 for little content. 

you can have anything in here and the content will scroll. the examples are social media links and lists. the lists can have an optional icon in front of them. to have the social media section, add 'social' to the class to get 
    <div class="t social">

template for a new social media link:
    <a href="[link]" title="[optional hover title]"><i class="[icon name] fa-fw"></i></a>
    
template for a new list:
    <li><i class="[icon name] fa-fw"><h2></i>subject:</h2> content</li>
    or
    <li><h2>subject:</h2> content</li>

to change the icon, go to https://fontawesome.com/icons. keep the fa-fw at the end to align them all.
